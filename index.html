GenAI Image Knowledge Generation

Part B: Creating and Sharing Your GenAI Image

Important Submission Requirement

For Theme 2 Assignment, you are required to create a GenAI-generated image that reflects your personal interpretation of the concept of corruption.

After generating your image:

  • Save the image to your device.
  • Email the image directly to your lecturer for projection and class sharing.

This step is a crucial part of the assignment and facilitates class discussion on diverse interpretations.

Steps:

  1. Reflect: Think about your understanding of corruption from the reading and reflection questions.
  2. Generate: Use a GenAI image generator (e.g., Google Gemini, DALL-E, etc.) to create an image based on your interpretation. Consider themes like abuse of power, economic impact, social erosion, or transparency.
  3. Save: Download the generated image to your computer or device.
  4. Email: Send the saved image as an attachment in an email to your lecturer. Include your name and student number in the email body.

This image represents the theme of corruption and its multifaceted impact on society.

Reference Citation (Harvard Style)

When citing a AI-generated image in your reference list, use the following format:

Google Gemini (2025) AI-generated image representing the theme "Corruption and its Impact on Society" [Digital image]. Available at: YOUR_IMAGE_URL_HERE (Accessed: DD Month 2025).

Assignment Requirement Reminder

This GenAI image and the associated translanguaging component (like the example text above) are part of Theme 2 assignment requirements. The image should capture the theme of your reading reflection or your personal response to the article on corruption, accompanied by text in at least two languages.

Remember to also include your written responses to the Session Reflection questions and the GenAI image citation in your final submission document.
